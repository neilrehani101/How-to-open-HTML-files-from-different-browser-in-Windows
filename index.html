How to open HTML files from different browser in Windows?

Step 1

Right click on your HTML file

Step 2

Click on "Open With" and then click on choose another app

Step 3

Then, click on "Chrome" or any other browser you'd like.

Remember to click on "Always use this app to open .html files.

Step 4

Press OK and you are done! Now, you can open every HTML file with any browser of your choice!
